Working Papers

Environmental Exposure, Health Investment, and Household Welfare

Md Al Amin. Job market paper candidate.

Abstract

I estimate the effects of environmental exposure on health investments and household welfare using quasi-experimental variation and linked environmental and socioeconomic data. The results speak to the private and social returns to environmental policy in settings where households face constrained adaptation options.

Climate Variability, Energy Use, and Household Adaptation

Md Al Amin. Work in progress.

Abstract

This project investigates how households adjust energy use and economic behavior in response to climate variability. The analysis will use weather, energy, and household-level data to measure adaptive responses and policy-relevant constraints.
